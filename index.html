ABOUT THE PRODUCT

Product Description

Click & Catch is a digital token-based claw machine booth where students claim tokens online and redeem them to play the claw machine and win prizes.

Problem It Solves

Campus booths often lack interactive experiences. Click & Catch provides an engaging claw machine game that attracts students and increases participation.

Target Market

The target market includes college students aged 17–22 who enjoy games, collectible accessories, and fun campus activities.

Unique Selling Proposition

Click & Catch combines digital token claiming, an exciting claw machine game, and instant rewards like customizable phone charms.
